Talking-Head Playback

Plays the TTS audio and the talking animation together, then eases back to the idle state the moment the audio ends. The audio and the video are separate files played in parallel — nothing is muxed into a single video (per the Milestone 3 spec).
idle
1. Make the audio: python tts.py "your text" -g male
2. Load the idle (or headshot), the talking clip, and that audio.
3. Press Play — the voice plays while the mouth moves; when the voice ends it eases back to idle. Press Stop to return immediately.

Talking-Head Playback (Flexible Length)

Load an idle headshot or looping idle animation, a talking clip, and an audio track of any length. The idle layer stays visible whenever you are not speaking. During playback the talking video loops its core segment (first/last 0.5 s trimmed) if it is shorter than the audio. When the audio ends, the player fades back to idle. Audio and video remain separate assets played in parallel.
idle
The idle image or video is shown by default. Press Play to start the audio and talking animation together. The first and last 0.5 s of the talking clip are skipped so mouth motion lines up with the audio. If the talking clip is shorter than the audio it will loop that trimmed segment until the audio finishes, then fade back to idle. Press Stop to return to idle immediately.
